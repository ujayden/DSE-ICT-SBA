The basic knowledge of networking in HKDSE.

Check List - Is that suitable for you?

  • Studing at any Elective (A/B/C/D)

Description

This section is designed for students who are going to take DSE ICT.

The ICT Networking Compulsory Part is an essential part of the HKDSE ICT framework. In today's rapidly evolving information society, ICT has become an part of our lives. Network is an important part of that. So, in this course, you will be learn…...

What you will learn

  • Networking and Internet Basics, such as network device and transmit technology (Wi-Fi)
  • Use of Internet, such as Email
  • Protocol, such as HTTP, DNS
  • Concept of Data Transmission and OSI model
  • Skills of answering questions

What you will get

  • Knowledge of Networking
  • Knowledge of Internet
  • Knowledge of Data Transmission
  • Knowledge of OSI model
  • Knowledge of Protocol
  • Exam skills
