Beginner - 1h 0m

Network and Internet Basics - Lesson 1

Description

When people talk about the Internet, they often talk about connecting with others, sharing information, and exploring different websites. But have you ever wondered how all of this works? How do computers send messages across the world? This lesson will introduce you how they connect and communicate with each other with some hardware.

Lesson Breakdown

Introduction to Networking: Understand the basics of what networks are and how they enable connectivity.

Matching Network Devices: Learn about various network devices and their functions in a network.

Final Quiz: Test your understanding of networking basics with a final quiz.

What You Will Learn

  • The definition and importance of a network.
  • How to identify and understand the functions of critical network devices.
