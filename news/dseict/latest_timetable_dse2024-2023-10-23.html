Latest Timetable of HKDSE 2024

23 October 2023

Quick Summary

  • The first subject is Visual Arts, on 9 April 2024.
  • The first compulsory subject is Chinese Language, on 11 April 2024.
  • ICT exam is on 27 April 2024.

The HKEAA has recently announced the latest timetable of HKDSE 2024. The exam will be held in April 2024.

For more information, please visit HKEAA website.
