NVIDIA has released GeForce RTX 4090 D GPU to the China market

28 October 2023

Quick Summary

  • RTX 4090D is the slower version of RTX 4090.
  • RTX 4090D aims to provide a alternative choice for users in China.

Nvidia has recently launch RTX 4090D, a slower version of RTX 4090, to escape from the ban of shipping RTX 4090 to China. Recently, United States Federal Trade Commission's (US-FTC) add export restrictions on certain AI GPUs, including, but not limited to, RTX 4090.

RTX 4090D use 5nm process, the core is AD102, but CUDA core, Tensor core and RT core are all cut down.
